NASA

Established on October 1, 1958, by President Eisenhower, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is the US government agency responsible for civil space exploration and aeronautics research. NASA has landed humans on the Moon, landed rovers on Mars, and explored the outer solar system, pioneering advancements in space technology.

ARMY

Indian Army Commandos, particularly the Para (Special Forces), are among the world's most elite warriors, known for extreme physical fitness, high-risk unconventional warfare, and a brutal 80% selection dropout rate. Beauty

Daily sunscreen (SPF 30+) is essential for preventing premature aging, even on cloudy days, as UV rays penetrate clouds. Key skincare habits include consistent moisturizing for all skin types (including oily) and getting enough sleep to reduce cortisol, which causes inflammation and dullness. Effective routines focus on cleansing, moisturizing, and protecting.
